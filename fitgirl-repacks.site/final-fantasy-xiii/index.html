Repack Features

  • Based on Final.Fantasy.XIII-RELOADED ISO release: rld-fifaxi.iso (59,388,428,288 bytes)
  • Final.Fantasy.XIII.UPDATE.3-CPY (2.4 GB) applied over
  • Optional fan-made Russian localization v02.07.2018 by FFRTT/ZoG Team added; notice – it replaces English texts/GUI
  • 100% Lossless & MD5 Perfect: all files are identical to originals after installation (when installed without Russian localization)
  • NOTHING ripped, NOTHING re-encoded
  • Selective Download feature: you can skip downloading and installing of English or Japanese voiceovers/videos packs
  • Significantly smaller archive size (compressed from cumulative 57.7 to 20.8~38.1 GB, depending on selected components)
  • Installation takes (for any single language): ~17 minutes on 8-threads CPU; ~30 minutes on 4-threads CPU; installing two languages at the same time is twice longer
  • After-install integrity check so you could make sure that everything installed properly
  • HDD space after installation: up to 58 GB (up to 62 GB during installation)
  • Use “Language Selector.exe” in game root to change the game text language; use official game launcher to change audio/videos language
  • Repack uses XTool library by Razor12911
  • At least 2 GB of free RAM (inc. virtual) required for installing this repack

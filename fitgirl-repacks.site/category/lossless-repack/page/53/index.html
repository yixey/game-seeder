  • Based on Superdimension.Neptune.VS.Sega.Hard.Girls-CODEX ISO release: codex-superdimension.neptune.vs.sega.hard.girls.iso (11,738,939,392 bytes)
  • Missing Deluxe Bundle content added: OST, wallpapers, artworks (551 MB)
  • 100% Lossless & MD5 Perfect: all files are identical to originals after installation (when installed with all options/languages)
  • NOTHING ripped, NOTHING re-encoded
  • Selective Donwload feature: you can skip downloading and installing of content you don’t need: bonus, Japanese voiceovers, full Japanese and Chinese localizations
  • Significantly smaller archive size (compressed from cumulative 11.5 to 2.3~4.5 GB, depending on selected components)
  • Installation takes 10-25 minutes (depending on your system and selected components)
  • After-install integrity check so you could make sure that everything installed properly
  • HDD space after installation: up to 14 GB
  • At least 2 GB of free RAM (inc. virtual) required for installing this repack
  • ATTENTION! If you’re installing this repack under Windows 10, run setup with compatibility set to Windows 7

A collaboration project between ASCII Media Worksâ€™ Dengeki Bunko and SEGA, the Sega Hard Girls are made up of various Sega consoles, including the Dreamcast, Sega Saturn, and Mega Drive! These anthropomorphized consoles are girls with an array of personalities and spunk, and this time they are teaming up with Neptune and IF for an all-new adventure!

Game Features

  • Introducing to the series, Hatsumi Sega, Dreamcast, Sega Saturn, Game Gear, and Mega Drive!
  • Give the team special stats and skills unique to their class using Class Change!
  • Maneuver through treacherous dungeon terrain by jumping, dashing, climbing or crawling!
  • Grab the Rainbow Gem in battle to unleash Fever Time! This reduces enemy turns during battle, enables transformations, and boosts stats by 10%!

  • All patches up to v1.10 applied
  • Extreme Injector v.3.6.1 for 2/3-core CPUs added to “bin” folder – run it before running the main EXE, if you play on Core2Duo/i3 or any other 2/3-cores PC
  • Modified unofficial RELOADED crack and ULC Unlocker added to NoDVD folder (available after installation). RLD crack wasn’t properly tested. Use it at your own risk.
  • 100% Lossless & MD5 Perfect: all files are identical to originals after installation
  • NOTHING ripped, NOTHING re-encoded
  • Selective Download feature: you can skip downloading and installing of multiplayer files (MP doesn’t work here anyway) and voiceover languages you don’t need
  • Significantly smaller archive size (compressed from cumulative 39.8 to 10.8 GB (SP-only files for any single language)), the maximum repack size is 14.8 GB – with all speech packs and multiplayer files
  • Installation takes (SP campaign): ~30 minutes (8-cores CPU + SSD); ~1 hour (4-cores CPU + HDD); 1.5 hours (2-cores CPU + HDD). MP files take another 10 minutes
  • After-install integrity check so you could make sure that everything installed properly
  • HDD space after installation: up to 40 GB (up to 75 GB during installation)
  • At least 2 GB of free RAM (inc. virtual) required for installing this repack

Selective Download

You can skip downloading of multiplayer and/or speech files you don’t need. Here is the list of selective files:

  • fg-selective-english.bin (also REQUIRED for Czech, Polish and other languages without their own speech pack)
  • fg-selective-russian.bin
  • fg-selective-brazilian.bin
  • fg-selective-japanese.bin
  • fg-selective-french.bin
  • fg-selective-german.bin
  • fg-selective-italian.bin
  • fg-selective-spanish.bin
  • fg-selective-mexican.bin
  • fg-optional-mp-files.bin (NO, Multiplayer will NOT be available with these file)

In example, if you want to launch the game with German UI/Subtitles/Voiceovers – skip all “selective” files, but download fg-selective-german.bin & all main files (01-05).

If you want to play in your language, but with English voiceovers, install the game with English files and then change the UI language in-game.

Installing the game with SP-only or with MP-files as well

In this repack some multiplayer files, not needed for SP-campaign, were moved to a separate archive – you can skip downloading and installing them.

But generally I wouldn’t recommend it. I’ve tested single-player campaign, of course and found no bugs or glitches with MP-files removed, but they MAY appear later in the game.

The probability is pretty low, but still. Since the size of MP-data is pretty small in this repack and installation is longer for just 10 minutes, don’t risk your game experience.
